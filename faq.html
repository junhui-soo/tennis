Frequent Ask Questions

What should I wear to my tennis class?

You should wear comfortable athletic clothing and tennis shoes with non-marking soles.

Do I need to bring my own tennis racket and balls?

You will need to bring your own racket for the class. If you don't have one, don't worry! We offer racket rentals and sales at our academy shop.

At what age can my child start tennis lesson?

We offer tennis lessons for children as young as 4 years old, but we recommend waiting until your child is at least 5 years old before starting regular lessons.

What if I've never played tennis before?

No problem! Our classes are designed to accommodate players of all skill levels, including beginners.

How long are the classes?

The length of the class may vary depending on the program, but most classes are typically 60-90 minutes long.

What happens if it rains during my tennis class?

In case of rain or inclement weather, the class may be rescheduled or moved indoors if possible.

What is the student to coach ratio in the classes?

The student to coach ratio may vary depending on the program, but we strive to maintain a low ratio to ensure personalised attention and quality instruction.

What happens if I/my child miss the group tennis lesson?

If you or your child misses a group class that has already been scheduled, it will be counted as an absence and no make-up class will be provided.

What is the cancellation policy for classes?

The cancellation policy may vary depending on the program, but typically requires at least 24 hours notice before the class to avoid being charged.
